Herding With Drones

In this modern age the farmer does not even have to be inside the field to gather his animals. The farmer can now be at home at his desk flying a drone to gather his flock of sheep.

With good control reaction the drone is agile enough to keep up with any animal. A farmer can elevate the aircraft to 500m in the air and see more the 10km in the direction he chooses. So now it is much easier to find a flock in a big area. Saving time and energy and even money as the drone does have a long time waranty and can be insured.
